Welcome to Heritage Farm!

We've been hand-raising guinea keets (chicks) for nearly two decades on our 150 year old family farm, just outside of McKinney in North Texas.

If you are an old hand at raising guineas, you'll find our keets superior. New to keets? We'll get you started!
